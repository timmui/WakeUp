WakeUp

Designed to keep you awake when you are in boring lectures.

View project on GitHub

Welcome to WakeUP!.

This is a simple app to keep you awake during those long lectures. Using the Myo, the app can identify when you are dosing off and it will try to wake you up by vibrating the Myo. After a few attempts at waking you up, the app will perform something that will definitely wake you up. Hopefully it doesn't have to get to that point.

Don't Be like him! Don't fall a sleep in class!
